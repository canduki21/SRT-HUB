Applications
Anorthosite’s unique chemistry — high aluminum, low silica, zero toxic by-products — serves six distinct industrial and research sectors.
Industries Served

Versatile Across Sectors

From lunar highland simulant to e-glass reinforcement — Greenspar products power cutting-edge applications across space science and advanced manufacturing.

01
Lunar Studies & ISRU

Premier terrestrial feedstock for lunar highland simulants. Used in ISRU experiments, planetary surface research, spectral calibration, and astronaut training scenarios. Compositionally analogous to Apollo sample anorthosite from the lunar highlands.

Recommended: Greenspar 2000, Greenspar 60, Greenspar 15
02
E-Glass Fiber

Greenspar delivers superior electrical insulation performance with a significantly reduced environmental footprint compared to conventional feldspar sources. Used by aerospace, automotive, and construction sectors requiring high-performance reinforcing glass fiber.

Recommended: Greenspar 2000
03
Paints & Coatings

Greenspar products reduce volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions while improving opacity, scrub resistance, and durability in architectural and industrial coatings. A sustainable alternative to conventional extender minerals with superior whiteness characteristics.

Recommended: Greenspar 60, Greenspar 45, Greenspar 15
04
Friction Materials

Anorthosite strengthens phenolic friction resins in high-performance brake pads and clutch systems. Its hardness, low free silica content, and chemical inertness make it a preferred reinforcing filler for automotive and aerospace friction applications.

Recommended: Greenspar 60, Greenspar 45
05
Polymers & Plastics

Greenspar enhances anti-block properties, UV stability, flame resistance, and structural toughness in thermoplastic and thermoset polymer compounds. Its platelet morphology and high whiteness improve surface finish in blown film and packaging applications.

Recommended: Greenspar 45, Greenspar 15
06
Mineral Wool

High aluminum and calcium content makes Greenspar an ideal raw material for stone wool and glass wool insulation manufacturing. Provides excellent thermal and acoustic performance with a lower environmental impact than competing mineral sources.

Recommended: AnoRoq, Greenspar 2000
