About Lumina
A pioneering material science company addressing today’s significant manufacturing challenges with unparalleled sustainability.
“We are a pioneering material science company addressing today’s significant manufacturing challenges with unparalleled sustainability.”

Lumina crafts specialized products through tailored chemistry and processing solutions, operating primary mining facilities 80 km west of Kangerlussuaq on Greenland’s west coast.

Our anorthosite — marketed under the Greenspar brand — is the only commercially available high-purity anorthosite product in the world, offering a sustainable alternative to conventional industrial minerals across e-glass, coatings, polymers, friction materials, and space research.

With no toxic by-products and an exceptionally low environmental footprint, Lumina is uniquely positioned at the intersection of industrial materials science and planetary exploration.

2013
Exploration Begins

Lumina’s journey initiated with exploration activities at the White Mountain anorthosite deposit, 80 km west of Kangerlussuaq on Greenland’s west coast.

2017
Construction at Qaqortorsuaq

Construction commenced at the Qaqortorsuaq site in Greenland, establishing the primary mining infrastructure and processing facilities for the Greenspar product line.

2019
First Mineral Shipment

First mineral shipment successfully completed, marking Greenland’s entry into the global specialty minerals market. Director of Operations Peter Madsen’s fourth mining venture in Greenland.

Today
Global Supply Chain

Lumina now operates a fully vertically integrated supply chain spanning three continents, with mining in Greenland, advanced processing in Jeffersonville, Indiana (USA), and operations in Mexico and Europe.

Greenland
Mining & Exploration

Primary mining at the White Mountain anorthosite deposit, 80 km west of Kangerlussuaq. Run-of-mine ore extraction, site logistics, and ongoing exploration.

United States
Advanced Processing

Advanced mineral processing facility in Jeffersonville, Indiana. Milling, classification, and quality control for the full Greenspar product line shipped to North American customers.

Mexico
Processing Capabilities

Processing capabilities serving Latin American industrial markets, enabling shorter lead times and reduced logistics costs for customers in the region.

Europe
Infrastructure Development

Ongoing infrastructure development to support European industrial customers, with a focus on e-glass fiber, coatings, and specialty mineral markets.

Community
Greenland Partnerships

Lumina is Greenland’s sole mining operation with strong community ties — committed to local employment, safety standards, and long-term deposit stewardship.
