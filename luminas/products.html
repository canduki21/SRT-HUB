High-Purity Anorthosite — Calcic Plagioclase Feldspar
Lunar 16A

Derived from igneous rock anorthosite (>85% calcic plagioclase feldspar), sourced from the near mono-mineralic Archean-aged Qaqortorsuaq deposit. Chemically stable with elevated Al and Ca, low Si, and minimal alkali (K+Na). The anorthite mineral (CaAl₂Si₂O₈) present gives this material the highest density and hardness of all feldspathic minerals, along with the highest refractive index. Bulk handleable with low dust and water content — suitable for mineral wool insulation and advanced industrial applications.
